------OVERVIEW------
As being the world’s highest mountain in the world, Mt.Everest is always been
the goal to climb once in a lifetime by professional and non-professional
mountaineers. Even the trekkers dream for Everest to see the highest wall, that
is why the most popular and adventurous trekking destination in Nepal is Everest
Base Camp Trek.This trek can be done by any age group (10-70) but may require
special guiding, therefore whenever you prefer to visit highest mountain in
Nepal better to hire proper guiding services with registered trekking
companies.Everest Expedition from Nepal is tougher than Everest expedition from
Tibet side because of avalanches and ice falls. Many people (Sherpa) have lost
their lives in expedition from South face by natural disaster. It is always best
to have experienced mountain guide and climbing Sherpa having similar mountain
experience.


------Itinerary------
Day 01: Fly to Nepal, Arrival in Kathmandu
Day 02 – 03: Preparation days for official documents and expedition preparation
Day 02: Fly to Lukla and trek to Phakding (2,652m)
Day 03: Trek to Namche Bazaar (3440 m)
Day 04: Rest day for acclimatization at Namche Bazaar
Day 05: Trek to Tengboche (3,870m)
Day 06: Trek to Pheriche (4,240 m)
Day 07: Rest for acclimatization at Pheriche.
Day 08: Trek to Lobuche ( 4930m. )
Day 09: Trek to Everest base camp (5364m)
Day 10 – 45: Climbing period
Day 46 – 50: Back to Lukla.
Day 51: Fly to Kathmandu
Day 52: Extra day in Kathmandu.
Day 53: Final Departure
